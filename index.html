Save The Day Artifact

  • Use 2-factor authentication when possible
  • Change your passwords frequently
  • Don't use the same passwords for everything
  • Don't share passwords with others
  • Use numbers, symbols, and capital letters in passwords
  • Don't use common phrases or personal information for passwords
  • Check the website URL/email address for suspicious top-level domains
  • Hover over hyperlinks and ensure their relevancy to the context
  • Don't download unknown/suspicious files
  • Only input personal information in trusted sites
  • Ignore any suspicious emails or messages
  • Don't share personal information publicly online
  • Don't post pictures of others without their consent
  • Don't post your current location
  • Don't post/engage in criminal activity
  • Turn on login alerts to get notified when someone logs into your account
